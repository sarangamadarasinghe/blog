Very Expressive Realistic Portraits

Black and white pencil drawings, beautifully executed. Architect and Designer Berikuly Erkin, really captures the emotions of the people he draws. His portraits vary in the quality of realism, but for me, one thing unites them, the way he is able to bring those emotions, expressed by a three dimensional real person's face, to a two dimensional sheet of paper. You can probably recognise some of the people depicted here and can appreciate how closely they resemble the face and expression of the real woman. Emilia Clarke - Daenerys Targaryen - Game of Thrones.
